History

Our museum dates back to November 19th 1798, making it one of the oldest museums in the Netherlands. Our museum has moved quite a bit since then. In fact, Amsterdam was not the first location, it was The Hague. in 1906 we have rebuilt a our hall for the nightwatch. These changes were eventually followed by more new additions like rooms for exhibitions. In 1906, we held our first major photographic exhibition. In 2003 we began a major renovation for the building which was finished in 2012. The Rijksmuseum has gotten more famous over the years leading to more than 1 million visitos anually, and with our range of unique objects and arts, you will always find yourself in a historic and unforgettable enviroment.
